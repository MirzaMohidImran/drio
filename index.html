How long does a web project take?+
Timelines vary by scope: a landing page takes 3–7 days, a full business website 2–4 weeks, and a complex web application 6–12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after our discovery call.
Do you work with Shopify and WordPress?+
Absolutely. We have deep expertise in both platforms. For Shopify, we build custom themes, apps, and headless setups. For WordPress, we develop custom themes, plugins, and high-performance architectures.
Do you build custom or use templates?+
We build custom by default to ensure your site is unique and perfectly aligned with your brand. We may use premium frameworks where appropriate to save time, but every design and build is tailored specifically for you.
Can you redesign my existing website?+
Yes — redesigns are one of our specialties. We audit your current site, identify opportunities, then craft an improved experience. We can also preserve your SEO rankings throughout the transition process.
What support do you offer after launch?+
Every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support. After that, we offer flexible monthly maintenance plans covering updates, backups, security monitoring, performance tuning, and content changes.
How do I get started?+
Book our free 10-minute consultation call. We'll discuss your project and goals. If we're a great fit, you'll receive a detailed proposal within 24 hours. No obligation, no pressure — ever.
What does it cost?+
We offer transparent, fixed-price quotes. Landing pages start from $500, business websites from $1,500, and web apps from $3,000. Contact us for a precise quote based on your specific scope and requirements.
Will I own the source code?+
Absolutely. Upon final payment, you receive complete ownership of all source code, design files, and assets. We're happy to sign NDAs. No licensing fees, no lock-in, no strings attached.
